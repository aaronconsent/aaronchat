Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information, and exactly how our text messaging works.

Last updated: July 21, 2026

This policy explains how Top of Class Marketing handles the information of people who visit this site, request a report card, or become clients. We keep it in plain English on purpose. By using this site or giving us your information, you agree to this policy.

1. Who we are

Top of Class Marketing is a marketing business owned and operated by Aaron Phillips in Livingston, Texas. Throughout this policy, “we,” “us,” and “our” refer to Top of Class Marketing. You can reach us any time at hello@aaron.chat or 713-384-8985.

2. What we collect

We only collect what we need to grade shops, answer you, and do the work you hire us for:

  • Contact details you give us — your name, business name, email address, and phone number, when you submit a form, request a report card, or become a client.
  • Public business data — information already public on Google and the web about home-service businesses (name, address, phone, reviews, ratings, website, photos), which our grading engine collects to build report cards. This is public data, not private data you gave us.
  • Basic site analytics — standard, mostly anonymous information about how the site is used (pages viewed, general location, device type), through tools such as web analytics and the Meta pixel, to understand what is working.
  • Messages you send us — the contents of emails, texts, form submissions, and chat messages, so we can respond and keep a record of our conversation.

3. How we use your information

We use the information above to:

  • Build and send your report card and the walkthrough you asked for.
  • Reply to your questions by email, phone, text, or chat.
  • Do the marketing work you hire us to do, and report on it.
  • Send you service updates, and, only if you have opted in, occasional marketing messages.
  • Keep the site secure and improve what we offer.

We do not sell your personal information. Ever.

4. SMS and text messaging

We text our clients and prospects two ways: from Aaron’s own phone, and through a business messaging service (Twilio). Both are covered by this section.

We will not share your mobile opt-in or your phone number with anyone. No mobile information — including the fact that you opted in to texts, and your phone number itself — will be shared with, sold to, or rented to any third party or affiliate for their marketing or promotional purposes. Text-messaging consent is not shared with any third parties, full stop. The only companies that ever touch your number are the messaging providers who help us actually deliver the message to you (see “Sharing” below).

How you opt in

We only text you if you have given us your phone number and permission to text you. That consent happens in one of these ways: you give us your number and agree to texts on a form or report-card request on this site; you tell us verbally or in writing that we can text you; or you text us first. We keep a record of when and how you consented.

What we text you about

Depending on how you engage with us, messages may include: your report card and its findings, replies to something you asked, appointment and walkthrough scheduling, account and service updates while you are a client, and — only if you agreed to it — occasional marketing or promotional messages.

Frequency, cost, and how to stop

  • Message frequency varies based on your conversation with us and the services you use. It is not a fixed number of messages per week or month.
  • Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier and plan. We do not charge you for texts; your carrier’s normal rates are between you and them.
  • To stop, reply STOP to any text at any time. You will get one confirmation that you have been unsubscribed, and then we will not text you again unless you opt back in. You can also just tell us to stop.
  • For help, reply HELP, or contact us at hello@aaron.chat or 713-384-8985.

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Text messaging may not be available on all carriers, and delivery is not guaranteed.

5. When we share information

We do not sell your information, and we do not share it for anyone else’s marketing. We share the minimum necessary, only in these cases:

Service providers who help us operate
Companies that deliver our messages, emails, and site — such as Twilio (text delivery), our email provider, our hosting provider, and our analytics tools. They may only use your information to provide that service to us, and are not permitted to use it for their own marketing. As stated above, your mobile opt-in and phone number are never shared for third-party marketing.
Legal reasons
If required by law, subpoena, or to protect our rights, safety, or property.
A business transfer
If the business is ever sold or merged, information may transfer as part of that deal, under this same policy.

6. Your choices and rights

  • Opt out of texts any time by replying STOP, and out of marketing emails using the unsubscribe link or by asking us.
  • Ask us what information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it.
  • Ask us any question about this policy and get a straight answer.

To exercise any of these, email hello@aaron.chat.

7. Retention and security

We keep your information only as long as we need it to do the work, meet our legal and record keeping obligations, and prove consent for messages we sent. We take reasonable steps to protect it, but no method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure, and we cannot promise absolute security.

This site is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.
