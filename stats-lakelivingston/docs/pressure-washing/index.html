This week's findings 3

gap

32% of Lake Livingston pressure-washing shops have under 10 reviews

Pressure washing is an add-on trade — most operators are landscapers or handymen who "also do pressure washing". That informality shows in review counts: nearly a third of local shops have no meaningful review signal. A pure-play specialist with a real reviews program dominates the category effortlessly.

opportunity

Boat-dock washing is the untapped lakefront niche

No pressure-washing shop in the local top-10 mentions "boat dock" on their homepage. Every Lake Livingston lakefront property has a dock that needs a spring wash. First shop to build a "spring dock package" page owns the search phrase from day one.

note

Category noise: car washes and gas stations in top-3

Outscraper's pressure_washing category returned car-wash retail (WhiteWater Express, Take 5 Oil Change) as top results — Google's category graph is polluted for this trade. Manual roster cleanup queued. Gap % numbers computed on all businesses so remain directional.
