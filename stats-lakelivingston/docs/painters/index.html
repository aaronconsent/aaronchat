opportunity

63% of local painters have fewer than 10 Google reviews

In a trade with only 11 shops in the whole Lake Livingston service area, review counts effectively determine the winner. LL Contractors leads with 102 reviews. #2 and #3 tie at 16. A shop that crosses 200 reviews owns the market outright — no shop currently is close.

gap

36% no-website + 63% low-review = a startable market

Even the biggest painter in the market has only 102 reviews and a basic website. This is a trade where an experienced solo operator can build the number-one presence in six months by doing what nobody else is doing: asking for reviews and running a real one-page site.

quirk

The market skews away from Huntsville — Livingston has 4 of 11 shops

Unusual: Huntsville doesn't dominate this trade. Livingston (4) and Point Blank (3) lead. Explanation: painting is a homeowner-relationship trade, and the lake communities' higher rate of second-home ownership drives repeat business locals want to service themselves.
