This week's findings 3

opportunity

43% of Lake Livingston lawn-care shops have under 10 reviews

Nearly half the market — 73 of 167 shops — has effectively no review signal on Google. In a trade where the buying decision is often "who does my neighbor use", the flywheel just isn't spinning. Any operator running the "text after every mow" review-request pattern crosses 50 reviews inside a season.

gap

36% of local lawn shops have no working website

Facebook business pages ≠ websites. Homeowners searching for weekly service from their phone bounce off Facebook logins. A one-page site with towns served, a form, and a phone number is enough — 60 of Lake Livingston's 167 shops don't even have that.

note

Category noise: Outscraper pulled some non-lawn businesses

The current top-3 by review count includes a public landmark (Sam Houston Statue) as a false-positive result from Google's lawn-care category tagging. The manual roster clean-up is queued for next build. The gap percentages above are still directional.
