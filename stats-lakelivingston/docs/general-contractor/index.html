This week's findings 3

opportunity

General contracting is the biggest addressable market — and the most under-reviewed

237 local shops, and 141 of them (59%) have fewer than 10 Google reviews. That's more shops with no review signal than the ENTIRE HVAC trade (91). In a market this fragmented, a contractor running a real review-request-after-every-job system moves into the local top-30 within a season.

gap

30% of local GCs have no working website

73 of 237 area general contractors rely entirely on Facebook, GBP, or a defunct Wix. For a homeowner researching a $50K addition, that's a hard disqualifier — no photos, no schema markup, no "who we are" page. Contractors who invest in even a one-page site with a portfolio out-position everyone in the search results.

quirk

Livingston (71) and Coldspring (16) punch above their weight vs population

Huntsville leads with 104 GCs (which tracks with population), but Livingston's 71 GCs is disproportionately high — reflects the Lake Livingston second-home / lake-house market where remodels are the primary spend category, not new builds.
