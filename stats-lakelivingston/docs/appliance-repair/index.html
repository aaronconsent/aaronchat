This week's findings 3

gap

46% of Lake Livingston appliance-repair shops have no website

Seven of 15 local appliance-repair shops rely entirely on their GBP + Facebook. Homeowners searching "washing machine repair Livingston" from a phone want to see brands serviced, warranty policy, and response window — none of which fit on a GBP.

opportunity

Brand-specialization is unclaimed territory

No local appliance-repair shop leads with a specific brand ("Whirlpool certified", "Sub-Zero specialist") on their homepage. Every branded appliance manufacturer publishes an authorized-repair partner list. A shop that appears on those lists AND names it on the homepage captures every warranty search for that brand.

quirk

Sears Appliance Repair still holds #2 — a legacy foothold worth studying

Sears Appliance Repair with 205 reviews at #2 is a fading national brand still dominating a rural East Texas category. That's a signal about how sticky "the name people learned to trust in the 80s" can be in service markets with limited competition.
