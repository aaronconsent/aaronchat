This week's findings 3

gap

34% of plumbers we found have no working website

Of the plumbers with an active Google Business Profile, roughly one in three still points to a defunct Wix, Facebook page, or nothing at all. For a homeowner searching from their phone with a leak, that's an instant disqualifier — the pro next to them with a real site gets the call.

opportunity

Median review count is under 50 — a real review push wins fast

The Lake Livingston plumber market is small enough that a serious review campaign (weekly ask, one-tap link, response drafts) can vault a middle-of-pack shop into the top 3 inside 90 days. Compare that to Houston, where the top plumbers have 800+ reviews.

quirk

Weekend availability is the differentiator, not price

Reviewers repeatedly cite Saturday/Sunday response over pricing when picking a plumber. Shops that hide their weekend policy on the About page instead of the header lose calls to whoever names the hours first.
