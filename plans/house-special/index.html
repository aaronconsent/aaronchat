The Questions Everybody Asks

Seriously, what's the catch?

No catch — but here's the honest part: we're betting that after 12 months of you watching the work, seeing the reports, and seeing leads come in, you'll want to stay on at $250/mo because it's working. If we're wrong, you walk and we eat the year. That's the deal. We only take on businesses where we're confident the bet pays off, which is why we screen carefully and only work with one client per trade per service area.

Who qualifies?

Service-pro businesses in East Texas — plumbers, electricians, HVAC, roofers, handymen, lawn care, cleaners, contractors, and similar trades. You need to be an established operator (we don't do brand-new startups) with at least a couple years of jobs and customers behind you. And you need to be the only one in your trade we're working with in your service area — first-come, first-served.

What if I want to leave before the year is up?

You can. Give us notice, we hand over everything (domain, hosting login, Google profile ownership, customer list, content), and we part ways clean. We'd rather know early than spend six months building something you're not going to use.

What's NOT included in the free year?

Paid ad management (Facebook Ads, Google Ads, Local Services Ads), lead-platform management (Thumbtack/HomeAdvisor/Angi), and the review-rocket postcard mailings are à la carte plates you add when you want them — month-by-month, drop anytime. The free year covers everything you need to be findable; the plates are what you add when you want to scale.

I already have a website. What happens to it?

Depends. If your existing site brings in leads, we usually migrate it to faster hosting and rebuild with proper SEO while keeping the URL structure intact. If it's an old Wix/GoDaddy/template site that isn't doing anything, we build fresh. Either way, you keep ownership.
