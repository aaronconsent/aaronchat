What's "AI Search Optimization"? Isn't this just SEO?

Related but different. Traditional SEO ranks you on Google's blue links. AI search optimization makes you the answer ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Google AI Mode give when someone asks "best plumber near me" or "who fixes garage doors in Livingston." Different content structure, different signals — and we do both.

How fast will I see results?

Local SEO compounds. Expect to see ranking movement in 60-90 days; full impact builds over 6-12 months. AI search visibility usually starts showing up faster (30-60 days) because there's less competition for the optimization techniques.

Will I be a Rank Me Local™ client forever?

Only as long as you find it worth it. No contract, no minimum term. The work compounds though — folks who stay 12+ months see the biggest wins because authority and citations take time to build.

Can I see what you're doing?

Yes. Monthly reports show every change made, ranking movements per keyword, traffic, calls, and Google Business Profile activity. Plain-English numbers, no fluff.
