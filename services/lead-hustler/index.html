Common Questions

Why Zoho CRM specifically?

Honest answer: it's the best-value CRM that fits service-pro workflows without enterprise pricing. Sales-pipeline view, multi-user, mobile app, automation, and ~$15/user/mo on Zoho's side. We've tried HubSpot (too pricey at scale), Pipedrive (limited automation), and DIY spreadsheets (you know how that ends). Zoho wins for the price.

What if I'm already on 4+ platforms?

The flat $499 covers up to 3. Add'l platforms are $99/mo each. Or we can audit what you've got, drop the ones that aren't earning, and stick to 3. Most pros find 3 is the sweet spot once they're actually being managed.

Does $499 include the platform fees / lead costs?

No — those still go to the platform (Thumbtack, Angi, etc.). The $499 is the management fee. Same pattern as Facebook Ads: we manage; the platform charges you for media. We do recover credits on bad leads, which often offsets a chunk of your platform spend.

Who owns the platform accounts?

You do. Always. We work in your accounts but you own them, the leads they generate, and the customer relationships. Take 'em with you anytime.

How fast will I see results?

Credit recoveries start hitting in week 2-3 (most platforms have 14-30 day dispute windows). The CRM and routing kick in week 1. Full optimization compounds over 60-90 days as we tune to your win rates.

Can I add another platform later?

Yes. Swap one out or add a fourth (+$99/mo). We'll let you know which platforms are earning their keep so you can make informed swaps.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yep. Month-to-month, no contract. We earn it.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Zoho CRM stays yours — full export, full ownership. The platform accounts are already yours. We hand off any documentation we built and step out clean.
