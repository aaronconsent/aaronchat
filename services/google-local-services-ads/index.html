Common Questions

Why is the minimum $2,000/mo?

Because LSA management is hands-on work: verification, profile optimization, dispute filing, weekly tuning, review monitoring. Below $13K in ad spend, 15% wouldn't cover the time we put in. The $2,000 floor keeps the engagement honest for both sides.

Why a 15% rate — and why cap it at $7,500?

15% scales with your investment without ever feeling punitive. The $7,500 cap (hit at ~$50K monthly ad spend) means that beyond a certain point, scale benefits you, not us. Big spenders get progressively better economics.

What's the minimum ad spend to get value?

Most service pros land in the $5,000-$15,000/mo range. Below $5K, LSA fatigue kicks in fast. Above $15K, you're hitting the 15% rate which feels more proportional.

How long does verification take?

Typically 2-4 weeks. Background checks add another 1-2 weeks for owner verification. We guide you through every step and stay on Google's case until it's done.

Who owns the LSA account?

You do. We manage it through Google's interface but the account, the lead list, and the "Google Guaranteed" badge are all yours. Take it with you anytime.

What if a lead's bogus?

We file the dispute. Wrong service, out-of-area, spam — all of it gets disputed automatically. Google credits typically come through within 5-10 days. Across our LSA accounts, we recover 15-30% of bad-lead spend this way.

Can I run LSA alongside Facebook Ads™ or Adwords on Google™?

Yes — and most service pros doing $10K+ in LSA also run those. They reach different audiences at different stages. We coordinate across channels so you're not paying for the same lead twice.

Cancel anytime?

Yep, month-to-month. The LSA account stays yours.
