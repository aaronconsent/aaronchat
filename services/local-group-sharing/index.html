Common Questions

Am I posting from MY Facebook account?

Nope. We post from accounts and personas already established in those groups, or from your business page where it fits. Your business name gets the credit but you don't have to manage logins or get banned for self-promo.

Will I get banned from groups for spammin'?

That's the whole point of doin' this right. We write helpful, conversational posts — not "CALL ME NOW!" ads. Each group has different rules and tolerances, and we know 'em. Real value first; the business mention is woven in.

How fast will I see results?

First posts go up in week one. Real traction usually shows up in months 2-3 as folks start recognizing your name. The compounding effect is what makes this worth the long-term play.

What if I don't have photos of my work?

We help. We can use stock or branded graphics until you've got real shots — or for the annual prepay plan we can pair this with a Built For You Website™ photo shoot.

Can I cancel anytime?

Why is this better than Facebook ads?

Ads target cold people. Local Group Sharing™ shows up in conversations folks are already having — neighbors recommending neighbors, town pages folks check daily. Warm, social-proof exposure that ads can't replicate.
