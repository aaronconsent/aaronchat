Common Questions

Why a flat 15%? Everyone else does "$199/mo" or "$999/mo" tiers.

Because honest is easier than complicated. Tiered pricing means we either over-charge the small folks or under-serve the big folks. 15% scales fairly with your spend — and you can see exactly what you're paying for.

What's the minimum ad spend I need?

$2,000/mo to make the math worth it. Below that, ad fatigue hits fast and our work can't earn its keep. If you're not ready for $2K/mo yet, try our Local Group Sharing™ first.

Can I scale up or down anytime?

Yes. Adjust your budget any month with 7 days notice. Our 15% fee adjusts with you — no surprise change orders or renegotiation.

Who owns the Facebook ad account?

You do. We manage it through Meta's Business Manager, but the account, the campaigns, the creative, the lead lists — all yours. Take 'em with you anytime.

Do I have to commit for X months?

Nope. Month-to-month. Cancel anytime. We earn it month by month.

What happens if the leads aren't coming in?

We share that pain — our fee scales with media spend, so if your campaigns aren't workin', we're working extra to figure out why. We've never had a campaign we couldn't fix; if we can't, we tell you straight up and pause the spend.

Is the creative included?

Yes — copy, headlines, images, simple video graphics, ad variations. If you want a custom video shoot, that's a separate one-off (we'll quote it).
