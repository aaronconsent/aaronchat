Common Questions

Are the postcards really included?

Yes — 100 postcards every single month at zero extra cost. That's design, full-color printing, addressing, mailing, and tracking. Most folks use them for review requests, thank-yous, and seasonal promos. Need more than 100? Bulk-rate pricing kicks in.

What platforms do you collect reviews on?

Primary focus: Google Business Profile (the one that moves rankings). Secondary: Facebook, BBB, Yelp, Houzz, Angi, Nextdoor — whatever your trade and customers use. We never violate platform terms — all reviews are real, organic, and incentive-free.

How do you handle negative reviews?

Instant alerts the moment one posts. We help draft professional response copy in your voice. We also build a "review funnel" that intercepts 1-3 star feedback BEFORE it goes public — so you get the chance to make it right first.

Will this get me banned by Google?

No. Google's policy: you can ASK for reviews; you just can't pay for them, fake them, or filter them by sentiment. Review Rocket™ does exactly what Google encourages — asks every happy customer, makes it easy to leave one, never selects who gets asked.

How fast will I see results?

First review requests fire week 1. First wave of new reviews usually shows up within 10-14 days (depends on your customer volume). 30-50% increase in monthly review count is typical by month 3.

What if I don't have a customer list yet?

We work with what you've got. CRM, spreadsheet, invoice software, even hand-written notes — we'll digitize and build your list. Going forward, Review Rocket™ captures every new customer automatically.

Can I customize the postcards?

Absolutely. We have ready-made templates for each campaign type (review request, thank-you, seasonal, referral, neighbor marketing), but you can request custom designs. We'll work with your brand colors, logo, and voice.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yep. Month-to-month, no contract. You own your customer list, your review history, and your brand voice — take it with you anytime.
