Office hours

Pricing questions, answered.

Are there contracts?

No long-term contracts — everything is month-to-month. Your grade goes up on the public scoreboard or you should fire us. That's the whole model.

What's the "ad spend separate + 10%" part?

On plans with ad management, the plan fee covers the work; the money that actually goes to Facebook/Google is yours and billed by them, and we add 10% of that spend for managing it. So a $1,000 ad budget = $100 management on top of your plan.

Can I switch plans later?

Any month, up or down. Most shops start at Enrolled (or the founding-five Honor Roll deal) and move up once the reviews and rankings start compounding.

How is this so much cheaper than an agency?

It's run by an AI-powered stack built with Claude Code — the same one that grades 1,900 shops a week — instead of a room full of account managers. You get the output without the overhead.

What do I actually get first?

Your free report card and a 30-minute walkthrough — before you pay anything. If it makes sense to keep going, we start on the plan you pick. If it doesn't, you keep the card and the fix list.
