Got Questions? We’ve Got the Answers

When folks first hitch their wagon to us, they tend to ask the same handful of questions. Here are the ones we hear most when we’re just gettin’ started.

Why do you have plans for marketing services?

Round here, we like to keep marketing as easy as pie—no guessin’, no headaches. You shouldn’t have to wander in the dark or get hit with surprise bills. That’s why our plans are laid out plain as day: you’ll know exactly what you’re gettin’, how it’ll grow your business, and what it costs—no back-door fees, no slick sales tricks. Mosey on over to our pricing page, pick the plan that fits, and we’ll saddle up right quick to rustle in real leads, pack your pipeline, and turn look-see folks into loyal customers.

So you’re tellin’ me there’s a set ad spend each month built right into these plans?

You betcha! Every plan comes with its own monthly ad budget—no need to haul out your wallet again or sweat over extra invoices. We set aside that cash up front for your Facebook, Google, or whatever ads we reckon will rustle up the best leads for your biz. You just pick the plan that fits your needs and budget, and we’ll handle the rest—from campaign setup to ongoing tweaks—so you’re gettin’ your money’s worth in clicks, calls, and customers.

Who picks up the tab for them yard signs and print materials?

Well, that’s on us! Every plan includes your yard signs and print stuff—no extra invoices or runnin’ around town. We handle the design, the order, and delivery straight to your door. You just tell us what you need—business cards, flyers, banners, you name it—and we’ll make sure your brand looks sharp out in the real world.

Y’all gonna cover wrappin’ all my trucks in decals, too?

Not quite, partner. While we don’t pick up the tab on vehicle wraps, we sure make it painless. We’ll handle the eye-catchin’ design, hook you up with our trusted wrap folks, and manage the whole shebang—so you get a sweet look without wranglin’ a dozen vendors.

What kinda lead count am I lookin’ at with the $249 Local Buzz plan?

In practice, you’re lookin’ at about 50 searches a month for your service in a market that size—give or take a few depending on the season, emergency calls, and your local crowd. We’ll corral those searches with spot-on ads, local SEO, and attention-grabbing profiles so you’re the first name folks see when they need what you’re sellin’.

With about 50 searches a month and a 5% conversion rate, you’re lookin’ at roughly 2–3 solid leads. That comes out to right around $100 per lead—still a small price to pay for a customer who’s already lookin’ for your services.

What if I already have a website?

Don’t worry none—if you’ve already got a site, we’ll pack it up and move it right onto our platform. Then we’ll tear it down and rebuild it from the ground up with fresh design, lightning-fast load times, and rock-solid SEO all baked in. You’ll end up with a slick, high-converting site that’s tailor-made to pull in leads and keep ‘em stickin’ around.

When I click the subscribe button, what should I expect?

When you click that Subscribe button, you’ll mosey right over to our secure Stripe checkout—just pop in your card details, click confirm, and you’re all set. You’ll get a payment confirmation email quicker than you can say “boots,” and our team will jump into action to kick off your plan. No fuss, no surprises—just a safe, straightforward way to get your marketing rolling.

How does subscription billing work?

It’s slick and simple. Once you’ve signed up, we charge your card each month on the same day—no need to lift a finger. You’ll get an email receipt from Stripe every time we bill you, so you can track every penny. Want to upgrade, downgrade, or ride off into the sunset? Just let us know before your next billing date, and we’ll adjust or cancel your plan—no hoops to jump through.

Are there any contracts or term based commitments?

Not at all, friend. We don’t saddle you with long-term contracts or tricky commitments. You’re free to cancel or switch plans anytime—just give us a holler before your next billing date, and we’ll take care of it. You stay because you’re lovin’ the leads, not ’cause you’re tied down.
