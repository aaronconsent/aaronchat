Nope. No contract at any point. The $250/mo Foundation is month-to-month. Add a Combo Plate the month you need more leads, drop it the next. Cancel the whole thing anytime.

So is the free year separate from the $250/mo plan?

Nope — they're the same thing. First 12 months are on us. Month 13 onward you pay $250/mo. One continuous service, two prices. No upgrade, no migration.

Does the $250 include my ad spend?

No — ad budgets (Facebook, Google, Local Services) are billed separately to your card so you keep control. We manage 'em; you set the dollars.

Grab a Side. From a $79 business card design to a $99 service page, you can order one-off items without signing onto anything monthly.

Do you really only work with service pros?

Mostly. Plumbers, roofers, electricians, HVAC, handymen, landscapers, cleaners — trades where folks search Google when they need help. If you're a service pro in East Texas, we're your people.
