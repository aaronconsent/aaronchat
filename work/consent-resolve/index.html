The brief

Consent Resolve is my company — I co-founded it and run marketing as CMO. It had to sell a genuinely new, compliance-first idea (warm-inbound visitor identification) while staying on the right side of TCPA, CIPA and the Texas TDPSA. Trustworthy, never creepy.

What we built

  • Designed and built the full marketing site (Astro → Cloudflare) around a locked “warm-inbound” story: identify a consented email, feed the client’s own retargeting, let the homeowner come back and call.
  • Reframed the compliance narrative around real U.S. contractor risk — TCPA, CIPA, and the Texas TDPSA — instead of generic GDPR boilerplate.
  • Built an interactive product demo (register → visit → consent) that emails the prospect their own “recovered lead” so they feel the product work.
  • Wired a consent-management autoblocker and mirrored every demo into an in-house CRM.
